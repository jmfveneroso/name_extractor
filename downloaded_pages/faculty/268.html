    This page lists, in chronological order, Ph.D. graduates from the School of Mathematical Sciences, together with their dissertation title, their advisor, and (if available) a link to their dissertation.

     

    The list does not include graduates from the former Department of Computer Science, which became the School of Computer Science in 2000.

     

    Furthermore, the entries until 1998 are only sporadic. We believe the list is complete in the period 1999-2009; please report errors and omissions to Nurit Liberman.
