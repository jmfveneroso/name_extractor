Academics

The Baptist School of Health Professions goal is to provide academic excellence through educational experiences by using the highest quality of technology, faculty and best practices. Whether you need help in studying, clinical practice, research for your paper, or access to the latest technology the Baptist School along with it’s outstanding Bruce A. Garret Medical Library has everything you need to succeed.
